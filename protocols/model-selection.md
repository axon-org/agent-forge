## Decision Factors
1. **Domain fit**: coding/reasoning vs writing/research.
2. **Reliability**: consistency on long-running tasks.
3. **Cost profile**: expected request volume and context size.
4. **Latency**: interactive vs batch workflows.
5. **Tooling compatibility**: function/tool calling quality.
